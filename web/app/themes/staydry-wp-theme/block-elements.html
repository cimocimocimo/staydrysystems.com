How it Works

The wall channels stick to your shower wall and rubber tubes are held in the sleeves of the curtain. The tube and curtain pressed into the channels creating the seal.

Learn more about the StayDry System.

Seals All Around

This kit includes 2 rubber tubes and 6 wall channels to enable you to seal both sides of your leaking shower. The reusable seal is created on both sides from the top all the way to the bottom.

Additional Info

The Deluxe Shower Curtain Kit comes with everything you need to seal both sides of your shower. The wall channels feature self-adhesive backings that allow the channels to be secured to your shower wall without the need for any tools. The two rubber sealer tubes fit inside the special sleeves on either side of our special StayDry shower curtain.
